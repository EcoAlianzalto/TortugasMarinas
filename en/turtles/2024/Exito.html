Hello! I'm Éxito, a very special Hawksbill turtle.

My godfather, Don Chuy, chose my name to reflect the effort in the conservation of our species. Although my sex has not yet been determined, I am happy to be part of a program that helps protect turtles like me.

I invite you to read about my story: from my first capture, through my first recapture, to my second recapture. Each experience has been an important step in my journey toward conservation.

A New Beginning

My adventure began on September 28, 2021, when I was captured for the first time. At that moment, I weighed 21.6 kg and measured 56 cm in straight carapace length and 42.4 cm in width.

This experience was crucial for me, as I was measured and recorded, giving me the opportunity to receive care and attention. It was exciting to be part of such an important conservation effort. It was just the beginning of my journey!

Growing in the Ocean

On December 23, 2021, I was captured again, and I was excited to see the changes in my body. I now weighed 22 kg, with a straight carapace length of 57.5 cm and a straight carapace width of 44.4 cm.

This growth is a great indicator of my health and well-being. Each recapture brings me closer to a healthy future in the ocean and shows that the care I received was paying off.

Evolution and Hope

On March 19, 2024, I was recaptured for the second time. How exciting it was! I now weigh 28.5 kg, and my straight carapace length has reached 62.6 cm, while my straight carapace width has increased to 48.2 cm. This growth is a great indicator of my health and well-being.

Every time I am recaptured, I feel that I am one step closer to being a symbol of hope for the conservation of my species. Thank you for continuing to support the protection of turtles like me!
