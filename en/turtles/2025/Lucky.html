A Strong Beginning

My story began on March 19, 2024, when I was captured for the first time. At that moment, I weighed 39.2 kg, with a straight carapace length of 71.2 cm and a straight carapace width of 53.6 cm.

This first encounter was essential to assess my health and officially include me in the monitoring program. Thanks to this effort, the first step was taken to follow my growth and contribute to the conservation of my species.

Growing Stronger

On October 14, 2025, I was recaptured, and the changes were clear. By then, I weighed 55 kg, with a straight carapace length of 77.5 cm and a straight carapace width of 58.2 cm.

This remarkable growth is a positive sign of my well-being and shows that conservation and monitoring efforts are working. Each recapture helps scientists better understand my life in the ocean and reinforces the importance of protecting our marine habitats.
